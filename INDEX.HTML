Less Scrolling, More Living. Digital Zen.

Digital Zen is a friendly app designed to help you find a healthier balance with your devices. We all know how easy it is to get lost in the digital world, missing out on real moments. Digital Zen helps you track your phone time, reminds you to take breaks, and even lets you focus without distractions.
